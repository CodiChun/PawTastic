About The Project

Paw Tastic is a free to use interface aimed at those who own a small pet grooming business.

What makes us unique is the simple and robust design of our website. Navigation between pages is simply done through a top navigation bar, and dropdown menus ensure a business owner will not run into any issues when inserting new entries into the database. The design has been tailor-made using the guidance of a client that owns a small pet grooming business.
